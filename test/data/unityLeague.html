Set up Companion App
  1. Open the Magic: The Gathering Companion App and login
  2. Go to the Play tab on the bottom navigation bar
  3. Click on Joining as followed by your name on top of the screen
  4. Enter your first and last name and Submit

From now on, when your organizer reports results from Companion, they will be correctly reported to your Unity League account.

Set up Melee.gg
  1. Open Melee.gg and login
  2. Go to Manage Your Account Settings
  3. Make sure Display username by default is not checked
  4. Go to Profile
  5. Click on Edit next to your First Name and Last Name
  6. Enter your names and Save

After changing your Melee settings, it will take 3-4 days until your results are fully transferred over to your account.
Please note, not all Melee events count for the Unity League. The green checkmark is shown once the first eligible Melee event is reported.
